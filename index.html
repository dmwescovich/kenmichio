Editorial Rationale

The digitization of Eugene Eddy's unpublished manuscript serves three foundational purposes: a) the preservation and digital presentation of the physical manuscript itself, b) the creation of a readable digital plain-text edition, and c) hosting a respository of critical texts which consider not only the analysis and interpretation of the text as an aesthetic work of fiction, but also the various questions that arise through the process of textual digitization.

Digital preservation of the manuscript was accomplished largely through high-quality image scans of each page of the manuscript which were saved as master files, and later processed for web optimization (courtesy of the IRIS Center at Southern Illinois University at Edwardsville, who granted access to the scanning equipment and image processsing software).
