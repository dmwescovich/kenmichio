The Manuscript

A digital facsimile of Kenmichio Goes Female

Introduction

This page contains a digital facsimile of Kenmichio Goes Female, a found manuscript, an unpublished novel written by an unknown St. Louis writer in 1934. Each page is presented as a digital image of the manuscript itself. Marginal annotations focus largely on the condition and nature of the manuscript as a physical obeject.
